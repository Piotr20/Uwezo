Travel across Africa

See some amazing places like casual

By Piotr Pospiech

Have you ever thought about going to Africa as a tourist? No? Why? I guess you just don't think you would feel safe. But actually do you know that you are less likly to be a victim of a crime in Africa than in Spain? In Africa you can not only see some unique things and also help someone. Does it sound great? When you go to Africa hire a guide he knows locals and some nice places to see and in this way you are allowing him to make his living with helping you. And there is possibility that he will save money and hire his brother than also save and buy new car and some years later he will have a logistics company

If you would like to buy some food buy it from such girl Yes maybe she doesn't have access to running water and is going everyday to some sort of lake to wash this bananas but they are still more healthy than this ones you are buying in your shop.

And simply enjoy African culture.
